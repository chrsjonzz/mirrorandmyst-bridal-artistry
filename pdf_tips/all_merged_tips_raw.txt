# --- AI/Brand/Expert Tips ---

## Skin Prep
- Start a gentle skincare routine 2-3 months before the wedding. (Brides.com)
- Hydrate your skin daily and exfoliate 1-2 times a week. (Bobbi Brown)
- Use a hydrating sheet mask the night before for a radiant glow. (MAC)
- Avoid trying new products right before the wedding to prevent reactions. (Brides.com)

## Foundation & Base
- Use a silicone-based primer for smoothness and longevity (e.g., Smashbox Photo Finish). (Pro MUA)
- Choose a long-wear, photo-friendly foundation (MAC Studio Fix Fluid, Estée Lauder Double Wear). (MAC, Estée Lauder)
- Apply foundation in thin layers and blend well for a natural finish. (Bobbi Brown)
- Use a lightweight, brightening concealer for under eyes and blemishes. (Charlotte Tilbury)

## Eyes
- Prime eyelids to prevent creasing (MAC Paint Pot, Urban Decay Primer Potion). (MAC)
- Choose waterproof mascara and eyeliner (Huda Beauty Legit Lashes). (Huda Beauty)
- Use individual lashes for a soft, fluttery effect. (Pro MUA)
- Opt for neutral or soft shimmer tones for timeless photos. (Brides.com)

## Lips
- Exfoliate lips and apply a hydrating balm before lipstick. (Bobbi Brown)
- Use a lip liner to define and prevent feathering. (MAC)
- Choose a long-wear lipstick (MAC Ruby Woo, Huda Beauty Liquid Matte). (MAC, Huda Beauty)
- Blot and layer for extra staying power. (Brides.com)

## Longevity & Setting
- Set makeup with a translucent powder (Laura Mercier, Charlotte Tilbury Airbrush Flawless Finish). (Charlotte Tilbury)
- Finish with a setting spray (MAC Fix+, Urban Decay All Nighter). (MAC)
- Keep blotting papers and a mini powder compact for touch-ups. (Bobbi Brown)

## Top Brands
- MAC Cosmetics: Iconic for foundations and lipsticks.
- Charlotte Tilbury: Glowing skin and bridal radiance.
- Estée Lauder: Classic, long-wear base products.
- Huda Beauty: Trendsetting eyes and bold lips.
- Bobbi Brown: Natural, skin-friendly makeup.
